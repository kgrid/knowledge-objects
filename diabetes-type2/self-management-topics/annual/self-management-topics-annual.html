Self-Management Topics At Each Annual Visit

Identification
Do you wear or carry diabetes identification?

Complications screening
Do you know (1) your results on screening tests?
(2) when you should be tested next?

Foot care
(1) What do you do to take care of your feet?
(2) Do you check your feet each day?

Injection sites for insulin
Do you rotate your injection sites around your abdomen and inspect sites?
